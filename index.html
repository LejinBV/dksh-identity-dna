Our values

Collaboration

Our culture

Our culture is defined by our purpose and values. To help us bring the culture to life, we use the People DNA. The People DNA defines the behaviors we use to live the values. They replace the 15 behavioral competencies we previously used. The People DNA is used in all people processes, from hiring to performance management, personal development, talent identification and rewards and recognition.
